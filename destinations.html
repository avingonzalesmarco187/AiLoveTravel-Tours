Explore Coron's Most Beautiful Destinations

From iconic lakes and hidden lagoons to coral-rich snorkeling spots and serene beaches, Coron offers unforgettable scenery at every stop.

Kayangan Lake

Famous for its crystal-clear waters and postcard-perfect viewpoint, Kayangan Lake is one of Coron’s most celebrated attractions. The lake is surrounded by dramatic limestone formations and offers a calm, refreshing swimming experience.

Clear Waters Iconic Viewpoint Scenic Swim

Barracuda Lake

Barracuda Lake is known for its unique thermocline layers and dramatic underwater rock formations. It is especially popular among divers and adventurous travelers seeking a different kind of freshwater experience.

Thermocline Layers Diving Spot Limestone Cliffs

Twin Lagoon

Twin Lagoon is one of Coron’s most iconic attractions, known for its turquoise waters and hidden lagoon setting. Surrounded by towering cliffs, it offers an enchanting and peaceful island escape.

Turquoise Waters Hidden Lagoon Iconic Attraction

Siete Pecados Marine Park

Siete Pecados Marine Park is one of the best snorkeling sites in Coron, known for colorful coral reefs and abundant marine life. It is an excellent destination for underwater exploration and reef appreciation.

Coral Reefs Marine Biodiversity Snorkeling

Coron Beaches

Coron’s beaches offer white sand, island hopping adventures, and moments of pure relaxation. Whether you want to swim, unwind, or enjoy scenic coastal views, Coron’s beach stops complete the tropical paradise experience.

White Sand Island Hopping Relaxation
